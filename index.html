About Palaparambil Stores

Palaparambil Stores is a highly trusted and dependable neighbourhood family supermarket situated near the Viyoor Bus Stand in the heart of Thrissur, Kerala.

Having served our community for decades, we have continuously customized our services around honesty, reliable service, competitive pricing structures, and excellent product sourcing networks. We pride ourselves on operating as both a premium grocery retailer and a key wholesale distributor.
